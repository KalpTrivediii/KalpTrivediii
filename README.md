# 💫 About Me:
I’m an analytics-focused software engineer currently based in London, with over two years of experience working on customer-facing systems and operational data problems.<br><br>In my current role at CKC Cares, I’ve been owning end-to-end analytical workstreams — from understanding business requirements to building SQL and Python-based reporting solutions that improve decision-making and operational efficiency. For example, I developed KPI reporting layers that reduced manual work by around 30% and improved visibility by 40% .<br><br>Before that, I worked as an AI Engineer where I collaborated closely with cross-functional teams to deliver data-driven solutions aligned with business goals, and led root-cause analysis workflows that reduced iteration cycles significantly .<br><br>What really stands out in my experience is my ability to work in ambiguous environments — I enjoy taking ownership, structuring problems, and translating them into scalable analytical or operational solutions.<br><br>Alongside my experience, I’m currently completing my Master’s in Data Science, which has strengthened my foundation in data modelling, analytics, and decision-making.<br><br>I’m particularly interested in roles like this at Wise because they sit at the intersection of data, operations, and customer impact. I’m excited about using data not just to analyse performance, but to directly improve how customers experience products — especially in areas like lifecycle communications and growth
